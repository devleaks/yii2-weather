A Pen created at CodePen.io. You can find this one at http://codepen.io/Enzo_Eghermanne/pen/NPByRG.

 A Weather App made with Jquery, Yahoo Weather Services and Yahoo Geolocation.

All props for the design idea go to Vincent Tantardini from UI8.

Instructions:
Click on the city to change it, and press enter (while the focus is on the city-input) to save and update weather data. You can also change the temperature scale (Fahrenheit / Celsius) by clicking in the top right corner.
